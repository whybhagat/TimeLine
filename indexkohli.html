Virat Kohli Biography: Birth
Family and Education
Age

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 to Prem Kohli and Saroj Kohli in Delhi, India
His father was a criminal lawyer while his mother is a housewife.
He was bred in Delhi's Uttam Nagar and attended Vishal Bharti Public School and Saviour Convent.
