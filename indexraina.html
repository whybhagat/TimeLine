Suresh Raina Biography: Birth
Family and Education
Age

Suresh Raina was born on 27 November 1986 into a Kashmiri Pandit family in Muradnagar, Uttar Pradesh. His parents originally hailed from Rainawari, a suburb in the city of Srinagar in Jammu and Kashmir. They left Rainawari in the 1990s amid the Kashmiri Pandit exodus
